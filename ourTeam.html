OUR TEAM

Jason Martin

Director

Unusually for this industry, Jason graduated with a degree in industrial engineering and had a career as a business analyst before he decided to explore his creative side and become a stylist. This balance of analysis and creativity means he has developed a unique problem-solving approach to hairdressing that allows him to design and style his clients’ hair to perfection. After working at one of Auckland’s top salons for almost 12 years, Jason opened Sadal & Co Hair Salon to bring the hairdressing experience back to being more about the customer, as opposed to the creative and artistic motivations of the industry.

Casey Taylor

Master Stylist

After gaining her stripes as a stylist at Servilles in Newmarket back in the early 2000s, Lisa subsequently moved on to work at top salons in Sydney and London over a period of 7 years. A self-professed obsessive compulsive, she can cut, colour and sculpt hair to perfection with effortless poise. Undeniably modest and humble, she is truly the definition of an artisan and master hairdresser. In her spare time she can be found straightening crooked picture frames and indulging her culinary ambitions preparing Italian and Asian masterpieces.

Kylie Louise

Advanced Stylist

Kylie is one of the brightest talents in the hair and fashion industry and has already built up an impressive portfolio of editorial work. Her work has featured in photo shoots online and in magazines such as Black, Remix, Fashion Journal and Italian Vogue. Her vision for on-trend fashion, ability to create seamless and beautiful colour work, as well as her unassuming and friendly nature means she has a bright future. In her spare time Jess can be found hanging with her Bijon-Shitzu Ollie and cutting shapes on the D-floor to electronic dance music

Melanie Coleman

Senior Stylist

Melanie personifies the saying 'anything is possible if you put your mind to it' . Having acquired a Beauty degree in Massey and beginning her career as a hair dresser two years ago. If you want a stylist with amazing attention to detail and a relentless commitment towards creating the best possible hair outcomes for her client's, Melanie is your go to gal.
